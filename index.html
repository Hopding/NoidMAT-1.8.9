What is NoidMAT?

NoidMAT is a Minecraft Forge mod designed to automate away tedious aspects of Minecraft's survival game mode. NoidMAT works in both singleplayer and multiplayer game modes. You can even use it on a completely vanilla multiplayer server!

What Does it Do?

NoidMAT is a work in progress, and features are constantly being added and improved. NoidMAT is currently able to perform three main tasks:

  1. Build walls
  2. Break down walls
  3. Mine out areas
